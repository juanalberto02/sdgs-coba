SDGS EXTRACTOR

SDGs Indicator Early Detection System

    The SDGs Extractor is an intelligent system designed to automatically identify and classify documents or publications based on their relevance to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). By utilizing advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques, this tool extracts key textual components—such as titles, abstracts, and keywords—from a document and compares them with a curated set of SDG indicators or rules.
