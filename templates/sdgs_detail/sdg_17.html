SDG 17

Partnerships for the Goals

Goal Summary

Strengthen the means of implementation and revitalize the global partnership for sustainable development.

Pillar

Governance, Economic

Back

Real Action

Building multi-stakeholder partnerships mobilizes resources and expertise for SDG implementation. Public-private collaborations accelerate innovation and financing.

Strengthening technology transfer, capacity building, and knowledge sharing enables developing countries to progress. South-South and North-South cooperation fosters equitable global development.

Monitoring progress through transparent data and accountability mechanisms ensures partnerships deliver results aligned with the SDGs.
